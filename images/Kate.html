  • Healthy Pumpkin Spice Latte Recipe
    Ingredients:
    2 shots espresso
    1 cup skim milk
    1 tbsp pumpkin puree
    1/2 tsp pumpkin spice
    1/2 tsp vanilla extract
    Brew 2 shots of espresso. Make milk foam as described above.
    Combine the pumpkin puree, pumpkin spice, milk, and vanilla extract in a pan. Heat on the stove and stir well.
    Pour the espresso and pumpkin mixture in a mug. Top with the milk foam.
    Skip the whipped cream.
